Important Guidelines

Avoid Recent Food Consumption

Do not upload any tongue image immediately after eating. Wait at least 30 minutes for accurate results.

Ensure Natural Lighting

Make sure you have natural lighting without specific focus. Avoid harsh shadows or artificial color casts.

Clean Mouth Before Capture

Upload image after washing your mouth when you have an uncomfortable feeling. This ensures the most accurate diagnosis.
